Adenike Grange

AdeGrange Child Foundation

AdeGrange Child Foundation is a charitable, non -governmental organization committed to addressing the challenges facing children and women in Nigeria and in Africa, through strategic interventions and advocacy. AdeGrange was incorporated under the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in Nigeria in 2009 and in the United States of America in December 2016. The founder of AdeGrange, Professor Adenike Grange has over the past fifty years gained a wealth of experience in Paediatrics and Public Health. She served as the Minister of Health of the Federal Republic of Nigeria (2007- 2008). Both as an individual and through her organization, she has formed strategic partnerships in order to make a global impact on child, adolescent and maternal health both locally and internationally. In recognition of the need to address the challenges of Universal Health Care as a prerequisite for achieving the desired goal of reduction in infant and maternal mortality with the overall improvement in life expectancy in the developing world, her current focus is on expanding her areas of activities in this direction through her membership of the Nigeria Universal Health Coverage Actions Network (NUHCAN)
